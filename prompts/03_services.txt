⏺ Done. The backend structure now follows standard layer separation:

  - controller/ — all REST controllers
  - service/ — business logic (including RiskScoringService,
  AIRiskSummaryService, AdminUserSeeder)

  - config/ — SecurityConfig, CorsConfig, OpenApiConfig
